Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Invasion Of Zombies ("the game") is built to respect your privacy. The short version: we don't collect anything. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no data ever leaves your device.

Information we collect

None. The game does not ask for your name, email, or any personal information. There is no sign-up and no login. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, cookies, fingerprinting, or third-party trackers of any kind.

Data stored on your device

The game may save small amounts of data locally in your browser (for example, your best distance or settings) using your browser's local storage. This information stays on your device, is never transmitted to us or anyone else, and you can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Network & offline use

The game loads its files from the web the first time you open it, and then runs entirely on your device. It does not send gameplay, telemetry, or any other information back to a server while you play.

Third-party services

The game itself integrates no third-party services. If you reach the game through an app store or another platform in the future, that platform's own privacy policy will govern any data it collects (such as download counts). We do not receive personal data from those platforms.

Children's privacy

Because the game collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children. It is suitable for a general audience.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date. Since we collect no data, we have no way to notify you directly.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@invasionofzombies.com.
